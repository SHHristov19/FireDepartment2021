We have committed ourselves on providing high quality fire and emergency medical services to our residents, businesses and visitors.

REACT Fire Rescue is a full-service, high performance, emergency service agency. Our history of dedicated service dates back to the founding of our community more than 75 years ago. Responding to current emergencies and remaining proficient with the skills required to prevent and suppress fires, deliver emergency medical care and educate members of our community in health and safety issues requires constant awareness and training.
